Key Patterns

What the data tells us

  • Recall is the bottleneck everywhere. Precision 0.77–0.94 consistently exceeds recall 0.55–0.81 across all configs.
  • Verifier hurts net F1. Gains 2–5 points precision but loses 3–10 points recall. Not worth enabling.
  • Codex reasoning effort is wasted. codex_xhigh (73.5%) ≈ codex_none (73.4%). Save the tokens.
  • SCHOOL is pathological. Zero support in ground truth, but models predict it → pure false positives.
  • Claude thinking mode has a fixable config bug. 96 of 225 failures are temperature misconfiguration, not model quality. Worth re-evaluating after fix.
